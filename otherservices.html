Other Services

At SP ENERGY, beyond solar panels, we provide off-grid solutions, hot water diverters, solar battery storage, and car chargers to save money on energy bills build a more sustainable future.

Huawei Luna 2000

The Huawei Luna 2000 is a solar inverter with advanced grid support features, ensuring efficient energy conversion and grid stability.

Huawei Sun 2000

Huawei Sun 2000 is a high efficiency solar inverter, maximizing energy yield with smart MPPT algorithms and grid support functions for stability.

MyEnergie Eddi Hot Water Diverter

My energie Eddie diverts excess solar energy to heat water, maximizing self-consumption and reducing reliance on ceonventional energy sources

MyEnergie Zappi Car Charger

MyEnergie Zappi car charger intelligently charges electric vehicles using surplus solar energy, optimizing self-consumption and reducing carbon footprint.
