Solar Power Grants for your Home

SP ENERGY is an SEAI registered company, and we make it easy for you to be able to money off your solar power energy installation.

What are SEAI Grants?

Through the SEAI Solar Electricity Grant, residential customers can claim up to 2400 back on the cost of a Solar PV installations.

Do I qualify for a grant?

Grants are awarded on a first come, first serve basis.
In order to qualify for an SEAI grant, you must be a home owner and your property must meet certain technical specifications such as

  • Roof Orienation
  • Roof structural Integrity
  • Roof position

How do I apply

To avail of an SEAI grant you must complete your application before the work can be carried out.

Once your grant has been approved we take care of the rest of the paperwork and get to work installing you new Solar PV system.

To start your application, visit seai.ie and follow the link for solar electricity grant.

From there, you can fill you in your details on the Grant Application portal, and select SP ENERGY as your registered installer.

Once the installation is complete, SEAI will process your application and refund you the amount due.
