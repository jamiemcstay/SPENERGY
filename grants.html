Solar PV grants

SP ENERGY is an SEAI registered company, and we make it easy for you to be able to get money off your solar power energy installation.

What are SEAI Grants?

Through the SEAI Solar Electricity Grant, domestic customers can claim up to €2100 back on the cost of Solar PV installations. Non-domestic customers can avail of up to €162,600.

How do I apply?

To avail of an SEAI grant you must complete your application before the work can be carried out.

Once your grant has been approved we can take care of the rest of the paperwork and get to work installing your New Solar PV systems.

To start your application visit SEAI and follow the link for solar electricity grant.

From there, you can fill in your details on the grant application portal, and select SP ENERGY as your registered installer.

Seai non-domestic pv grants up to €162,600

seai domestic grants up to €2100

Once the installation is complete, SEAI will process your application and refund you the amount due.
